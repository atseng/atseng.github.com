Salvaged Future 5 Days Exhibit About

  • Est. in the Summer of 2012

    Art Act came to fruition through dialogue
    between artist Allen Tseng
    & activist Dylan Irie
    Scharf-Lazerow

  • Activists strive to implement
    progressive policies. The problem is they end up suffering from the constant anxiety of waiting to see if their contributions and hard work have made an impact. This can be often frustrating and exhausting.

  • To progress forward, on the collective level, it is important
    for the individual to rejuvenate
    their spirit and mind. Art allows
    the capacity to create immediate change in the physical space around us. This process is an effective and therapeutic medium: to inspire, to be a catalyst for the mind; in that moment, a vision of
    a more hopeful tomorrow is made real.

  • Art Act provides participants
    the space and opportunity to perform and contribute. Through collaboration, artists and activists persist to stimulate engaging discourse and create innovative perspectives.

  • We are ART ACT,
    an artist and activist collaborative
    that designs stimulating and inspiring spaces to empower and
    bring individuals & communities together
    for greater awareness, healing, and
    transformation.
